About Us

Majestic Studio Established in 2001 in Khartoum, Sudan, Majestic Studio excels in photography and videography, starting with weddings and expanding to Cairo (Sheikh Zayed). We cover products, graduations, portraits, and birthdays. Famous as "Majestic" in Sudan, we aim to shine in Egypt. Services include printing, canvas prints, artwork, podcast production, and studio rentals with/without equipment, delivering quality with our expertise.
